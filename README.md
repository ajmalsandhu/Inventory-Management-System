# Inventory-Management-System

An Inventory Management System built in Microsoft Excel that helps track products, vendors, customers, purchases, and sales while providing an interactive dashboard for monitoring business performance.

The system allows users to manage inventory data efficiently and view real-time insights through KPIs, charts, and automated notifications.

**Project Overview**

This project demonstrates how Excel can be used to build a complete inventory tracking and analytics system. It enables businesses to record product details, track purchase and sales transactions, manage vendor and customer information, and monitor stock levels.
The dashboard provides a centralized view of key business metrics such as total purchase amount, total sales amount, stock value, and overall profit or loss.

1. Inventory Management

Monitor product availability

2. Vendor Management

Track procurement data

3. Customer Management
Maintain customer records

4. Purchase & Sales Tracking
Record product purchases
Track product sales
Calculate total purchase and sales values

5. Interactive Dashboard

6. Automated Low Stock Notifications

A notification panel alerts users when product stock falls below 5 units, helping ensure timely reordering and preventing stock shortages.
